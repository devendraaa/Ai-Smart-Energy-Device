Process

How the system works

01
Continuous Area Monitoring

The AI module constantly scans the area using occupancy detection sensors, building an accurate real-time presence map.

02
Presence Confirmed — Fan On

When people are detected, the fan runs at normal speed. Comfort is never compromised when the space is occupied.

03
No Occupancy — Fan Off

When the area is detected as empty, the device automatically reduces speed or switches the fan off, eliminating waste.

04
Instant Resume

The moment someone re-enters, the fan resumes normal operation automatically — no switches, no delays.
