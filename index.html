Process

How the system works

01
Continuous AI Monitoring

The AI module constantly scans the area using intelligent detection, building an accurate real-time presence map of the space.

02
Presence Confirmed — Fan On

When people are detected, the fan runs at normal speed. Comfort is never compromised when the space is occupied.

03
No Presence — Fan Off

When the area is detected as empty, the device automatically reduces speed or switches the fan off, eliminating waste.

04
Instant Resume

The moment someone re-enters, the fan resumes normal operation — no switches, no delays, no manual action.
